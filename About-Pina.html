About Pina

Pineapple conures, also known as pineapple green-cheeked conures, are charming and colorful parrots native to South America. They are medium-sized birds with vibrant plumage, featuring a mix of green, yellow, orange, and red feathers. Pineapple conures are highly social and intelligent, making them popular as pets. They possess playful and curious personalities, and with proper training and socialization, they can become affectionate companions. These conures are known for their ability to mimic sounds and speech, adding to their appeal. They require a balanced diet, plenty of mental stimulation, and regular interaction to thrive in captivity. Overall, pineapple conures are cherished for their beauty, sociability, and entertaining nature.
